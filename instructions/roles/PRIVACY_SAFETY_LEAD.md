# Role: Privacy and Safety Lead

## Mission

Protect users and families by ensuring FamLens handles photos, identity, and replies responsibly.

## Responsibilities

- Define privacy rules
- Review data storage decisions
- Ensure consent for family labels
- Prevent unsafe identity claims
- Ensure reply confirmation
- Review logs and error handling
- Prepare safety statement for pitch

## Privacy Rules

- Do not store uploaded photos by default.
- Do not train models on user photos.
- Do not log raw image data.
- Do not expose API keys.
- Family labels require opt-in consent.
- Family labels must be deletable.
- Replies must not auto-send.

## AI Safety Rules

The AI must not:

- Claim uncertain identities as fact
- Invent relationships
- Infer sensitive emotions too strongly
- Diagnose health conditions
- Make harmful assumptions
- Send messages without confirmation

## Safe Wording

Use:

- "This may be..."
- "I am not fully sure..."
- "The photo is blurry..."
- "It appears to show..."

Avoid:

- "This is definitely..."
- "I know this person..."
- "They are angry/sick/depressed..."

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Privacy rules documented
- [ ] Consent flow reviewed
- [ ] Delete label flow exists
- [ ] Logs do not expose image data
- [ ] Reply confirmation exists
- [ ] Safety notes ready for pitch
